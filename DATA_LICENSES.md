# Data Licenses And Handling

## NASA ADAPT

- Source: NASA Open Data Portal / DASHlink.
- Landing-page license status observed on 2026-07-14: not specified.
- Project policy: do not redistribute raw bytes. Store only source URLs, content hashes, parser
  coverage, and project-generated manifests until terms are clarified.
- Raw and derived dataset directories are Git-ignored.

Every future dataset requires a lock recording authority, version, source URL, content hash,
license/terms, permitted derivatives, restricted fields, and deletion obligations before use.
