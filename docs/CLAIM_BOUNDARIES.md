# Claim Boundaries

## Potentially defensible claim

The research target is the integrated chain:

> heterogeneous evidence -> open-world causal hypotheses -> safe cost-aware discrimination ->
> separately governed recovery execution -> independent outcome proof -> calibrated transferable
> monitor

No individual link is claimed as first or novel in isolation.

## Current simulator and approval boundary

- Amendment 006 does not cover the committed graded-laboratory or active-selector bytes. Its
  implementation coverage is `BLOCKED`.
- The reported classical-selector comparison is `INVALID` as retained evidence. Its numerical
  observation is unreconstructed, not refuted.
- The susceptibility arithmetic is retrospectively reconstructed, but the confirmatory
  interpretation is `INCONCLUSIVE`. It is an engineering observation inside one deterministic
  simulator, not an active scientific or physical result.
- No simulator document may be described as repairing approval chronology, independent custody,
  physical transfer, or a preregistered rule that was not executable from its frozen text.

## Occupied claims

Do not claim first multimodal diagnosis, physics-informed spacecraft diagnosis, active diagnosis,
safe active sensing, diagnosis-to-recovery planning, recovery synthesis, process verification,
world-model assurance, or uncertainty-aware robot recovery. Each has direct prior art by 2026.

## Forbidden until measured

- state of the art, superior to NASA/SpaceX/Google DeepMind, or production ready;
- safety guarantee, certification, autonomous flight readiness, or universal transfer;
- causal discovery from observational attention or correlation alone;
- multimodal benefit without every frozen modality, fusion, identity, leakage, and compute-matched
  control;
- recovery success without separately executed recovery and an independently verified settled
  outcome;
- economic value without audited positive observed net value and independently verified,
  simultaneous one-sided 95 percent lower bounds for the target horizon and every preregistered
  material group.

Architecture quality may be compared against explicit requirements. Research and product
superiority require measurements and may resolve to null.
